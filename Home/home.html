What We Do

The Learning Centre (LFSLC) offers a variety of resources to help students, instructors, faculty, and staff within The Faculty of Land and Food Systems achieve excellence in learning and teaching. Our mission is to enable all LFS constituents to reach their learning and teaching, communication and research goals enhanced by technology. The Learning Centre provides support and training on pedagogy, learning, classroom techniques, communication, and technologies that allow individuals to take risks, innovate, and try new things in their teaching and research.
