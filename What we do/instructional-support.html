The Learning Centre is here to help you with your instructional support needs.

We can help:

  • troubleshoot Connect or Canvas

  • copy and prep your course at the beginning of term

  • manage student enrolment

  • consult on course design

  • assist with any of UBC’s learning technology tools

Who can use this service?

All instructors, including TAs, in the Faculty of Land and Food Systems are welcome to come to us for assistance. We are able to provide some support for students, depending on the case.
