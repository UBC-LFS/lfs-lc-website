The Learning Centre offers assistance various IT-related matters

Desktop, laptop and mobile device

  • Setting up new devices

  • Installing UBC licensed software

  • Encryption and key escrow

  • Diagnostics and repair recommendations

  • Print services setup and troubleshooting

  • Data recovery and backup

Email and web

  • FASmail support (All LFS Faculty, staff, graduate student and student staff are eligible for an account)

  • Assistance with blog, CMS or web site setup and hosting (see Web Support for more info)

Video Conferencing Assistance

  • Bluejeans conference call setup

  • Consultation on conferencing implementation and strategies

  • PA and projection equipment setup

Servers and Storage

  • Cloud based virtual servers hosted on EduCloud at UBC

  • Central hosting of research servers in the University Data Centre (UDC)

  • Online storage for staff and research groups

  • UBC Workspace support

Computing Facilities

  • MCM 192 - 10 seat Windows computer lab with additional space for your own machine

  • MCM 194 - 6 seat Windows computer lab with additional space for your own machine

  • MCM 266 - Digital Media Lab for content creation

Who can use this service?

We have IT staff available 9 to 5 from Monday to Friday. Staff, faculty, and students can drop into to see for assistance during normal working hours, or we can come onsite to their offices, labs or locations. Remote assistance is also available.
