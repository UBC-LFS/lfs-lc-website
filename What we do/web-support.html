The Learning Centre can help you create and maintain a strong web presence by leveraging UBC's Common Look and Feel (CLF) to ensure that your website is easy to recognize as part of UBC.

Support for every stage of the web development process.

We can help:

  • set up your website on UBC's Content Management System

  • ensure that your website is aligned with the UBC Common Look and Feel

  • link Google Analytics to your website so that you can measure site performance

  • review your existing site and suggest ways to improve usability

  • create custom interactive components to your specification (on a case-by-case basis)

Who can use this service?

The Learning Centre's web support services are offered to anyone in the faculty, but only UBC Faculty and Staff are permitted to use the UBC WordPress CMS. We're happy to help troubleshoot your non-UBC sites.
